Hej! *pron: [ˈhɛj]


Just like a snowflake is organic, created in a super cool environment – it appears white – but actually it reflects the whole spectrum of light. It's irregular but looks almost like perfect. Each snowflake is created unique in shape, size, pattern and structure. That's how I like to see user experience and people – every case is unique. Create unique.

I was born Sweden. Always with a curious mind. If it's not exploring new yoga poses or moutains for skiing, it's observing behaviors and situations.

I have worked in the tech industry for over 6 years and gained experience being employed in both early stage start-ups and larger coorperation in San Francisco, Paris and Stockholm. I had the chance to collaborate with some incredibly inspiring and talented people.

While I was working as a Product Marketing Manager, driving global feature & product launches, I understood that my interest in talking to users and understanding their painpoints was something real. That's what drove me to User Experience Design.
